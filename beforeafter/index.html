Almost any portrait will benefit from some digital retouching and enhancement. I will do some retouching as removing minor blemishes and correcting color imbalances to every picture, but if you want I can also remove/fade away wrinkles, age spots, scars or any other unwanted items to reveal your genuine beautiful younger you. Think of it as a makeup - there is nothing wrong with wearing makeup as long as you are the one, who attracts the attention, not the makeup itself. That's why I always go for the natural look, since as you know "LESS IS MORE"!
